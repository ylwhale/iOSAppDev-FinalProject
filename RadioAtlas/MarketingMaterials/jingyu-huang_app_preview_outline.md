# RadioAtlas App Preview Outline (30 seconds max)

**0–4s**  Launch splash + onboarding first screen  
Text callout: *Listen to the World*

**4–10s**  Places tab  
Show browsing categories and opening a place card.

**10–18s**  Map tab  
Tap pins, trigger the random-station flyover, and land on a radio station.

**18–24s**  Playback  
Show the mini player buffering, then playing a live stream.

**24–30s**  Favorites / Recents  
Heart a station, switch tabs, and show that the listening flow persists.

Suggested voice-over:  
“RadioAtlas helps you explore places, discover radio from around the world, and keep your listening flow organized wherever you go.”
